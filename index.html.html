Payment Guide
Pakistani freelancers ke liye step-by-step tarika — Fiverr, Upwork, X se Pakistan mein paise kaise layein
1

Payoneer Account Banayein (Free)

Payoneer Pakistani freelancers ka sabse best dost hai. Fiverr aur Upwork dono directly Payoneer ko support karte hain. payoneer.com par account banayein — CNIC aur bank details chahiye hongi.

💡 Fiverr ka referral link use karein — dono ko bonus milta hai
2

Fiverr → Payoneer Link Karein

Fiverr account mein Settings → Billing → Add Payout Method → Payoneer select karein. Ek baar link hone ke baad har order ka paisa automatically Payoneer mein aayega. Minimum withdrawal $20 hai.

💡 14-day clearance period hota hai — order complete ke 14 din baad available hota hai
3

Upwork → Payoneer ya Wise

Upwork mein Settings → Get Paid → Add Payment Method. Payoneer ya Wise dono kaam karte hain. Wise mein exchange rate zyada acha milta hai — $1 = market rate milta hai bina hidden fees ke.

💡 Wise prefer karein agar amount $200+ hai — zyada faida milega
4

X / Twitter Monetization

X Creator Revenue Share aur Tips ke liye Stripe account chahiye, jo Pakistan mein directly available nahi. Payoneer ka virtual US bank account use karein — Payoneer US routing number milta hai jo Stripe mein add kar sakte hain.

💡 Payoneer → Global Payment Service → US Bank Details — ye US account number Stripe mein use karein
5

Payoneer se Pakistani Bank Mein Transfer

Payoneer account mein paisa aane ke baad: Payoneer → Withdraw → Local Bank Transfer choose karein. Apna HBL, UBL, MCB, ya Meezan Bank account add karein. 1-3 business days mein PKR mein convert hokar aata hai.

💡 Meezan Bank best exchange rate deta hai Payoneer ke liye
6

Tax aur FBR — Remittance Certificate

Pakistan mein freelancing income pe tax lagta hai agar $1200/year se zyada ho. Payoneer se Remittance Certificate download karein FBR ke liye. Freelancers ke liye SRO tax exemption bhi available hai — FBR portal check karein.

💡 Payoneer transactions ka record rakhein — annual FBR filing ke waqt zarurat padegi
